About Me

I'm a passionate web developer with a keen eye for design and a commitment to creating beautiful, functional websites. With expertise in HTML and CSS, I bring ideas to life through clean, efficient code and modern design principles.

I have completed my SSC from Bangladesh and hold a diploma in MERN stack web development. Additionally, I am an ethical hacker with a strong background in computer networking, cybersecurity, and ethical hacking practices.
